How does the computer solve it?

Flood Fill (BFS) spreads out evenly in every direction, like water filling the maze. Because it explores the closest cells first, the moment it reaches the goal it has found the shortest path.

Wander (DFS) follows one path as far as it can, and only backs up when it hits a dead end. It's fast but the path it finds may be the long way around!
